Symphony Daily Devotions App

Every weekday, Symphony Church records a short reading and reflection of a book in the Bible, called a daily devotional. The idea for the Symphony Daily Devotions app stemmed from the want for an easier way to listen to the daily devotional on the go. Previously, daily devotions were only accessible through a service which required users to call in to hear the day's recording. Now users can listen to the current week's devotions at the press of a button. So simple...so easy! Why didn't I think of that?
